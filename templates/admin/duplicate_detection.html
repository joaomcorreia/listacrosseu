Duplicate Detection Guidelines:

  • Exact Duplicates: Identical name, city, and address - usually safe to remove duplicates
  • Name Duplicates: Same name, different addresses - could be legitimate different locations (e.g., multiple McDonald's branches)
  • Address Duplicates: ‚ö†Ô∏è Use extreme caution! Different businesses at the same address are often legitimate:
    • Shopping malls with multiple stores
    • Office buildings with different companies
    • Food courts with various restaurants
    • Business centers with multiple tenants
    Only remove if businesses have very similar names AND you're certain they're duplicates.
  • Always verify before removing - when in doubt, keep both businesses
  • Check business details by clicking the Business ID links
  • Look for patterns: All same owner, same creation date, identical categories might indicate import duplicates
